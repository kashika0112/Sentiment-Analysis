About We Care

Let the worry fly and serenity pave in!

In the world of social media, there are innumerable opinions circulating the Internet targeting people based on sexuality, career, background, etc. These criticisms though baseless have a profound impact on our mental health. Statistics have shown that young users of social media have a substantially high rate of reported depression (13 to 66 %). Platforms like Twitter are being maliciously utilized to spread hatred and bigotry.
To make ourselves more aware and to tackle depression amongst youngsters, we have come up with the idea of “We Care - Let the worry fly and serenity pave in”, a website aimed at reducing stress levels. It includes a feature of sentiment analysis that allows users to analyze hashtags trending on Twitter. We plan to extract 2500 tweets using the Twitter Developer APIs and train them using the Naive Bayes Algorithm to categorize tweets into positive, negative, and neutral. The modules used would include sklearn, tweepy, numpy, pandas, regex, and matplotlib. We believe that this will ensure that the safety, inclusion, and respect of all communities is not compromised.
